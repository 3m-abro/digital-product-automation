## PHASE 1 — FORM THE LLC (Week 1, ~3 hours total)

### Step 1: Register with Northwest Registered Agent
**URL:** northwestregisteredagent.com
**Time:** 20 minutes
**Cost:** $39/year (agent) + $100 (Wyoming state fee) = ~$139

```
Fill in:
  Company name:     Abro Digital LLC
  State:            Wyoming
  Member name:      Maqsood Muhammad Mujtaba Abro
  Member address:   Your Malaysian address is fine
  Registered agent: Northwest (they handle this)
```

Northwest files with Wyoming on your behalf.
You receive your Certificate of Organization by email
within 1-3 business days.

**Documents you'll receive:**
- Certificate of Organization (the LLC is born)
- Operating Agreement (keep this — banks need it)
- Registered Agent Agreement

---

### Step 2: Get Your EIN (US Tax ID)
**Time:** 20-30 minutes on the phone
**Cost:** Free
**When:** Same day or day after LLC certificate arrives

```
Call IRS International Line:
  Number:  +1-267-941-1099
  Hours:   Monday–Friday, 6:00 AM – 11:00 PM Eastern
  
  Malaysia time conversion:
  6 AM Eastern  = 7 PM Malaysia  ✅ (after work)
  11 PM Eastern = 12 PM Malaysia ✅ (lunch break)

Have ready when they answer:
  - LLC name: Abro Digital LLC
  - LLC state: Wyoming
  - Your full name: Maqsood Muhammad Mujtaba Abro
  - Your address (Malaysian address is fine)
  - Reason for EIN: "Started a new LLC, need EIN for banking"
  - Your SSN/ITIN: You won't have one — tell them you're
    a foreign national without an SSN
    They will issue EIN to foreign nationals by phone ✅

They give you the EIN verbally on the call.
Ask them to mail the confirmation letter (CP-575).
Write down the EIN immediately.
```

**Alternative if phone doesn't work:**
- Form SS-4 by fax: 4-6 weeks
- EIN service (e.g. IRS-EIN-Tax-ID.com): ~$79, 1-2 weeks

  Form 1120 (US Corporation Tax Return)
  Form 5472 (Foreign-Owned US Disregarded Entity)

Even if you owe ZERO tax — you must still file.
This is non-negotiable. Budget for it from day one.

```
"I am a Pakistani national living in Malaysia on
an Employment Pass. I formed a Wyoming single-member
LLC (Abro Digital LLC) and I sell digital products
on Etsy, Gumroad, and Amazon KDP. All buyers are
international — I have no US customers, no US office,
no US employees. I want to confirm:
1. Whether my income qualifies as non-ECI
2. What US filings are required
3. Whether the US-Pakistan tax treaty applies
4. What I owe, if anything"
```
